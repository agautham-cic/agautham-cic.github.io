# 3. MIS for CI

The second half of the 20th century and early 21st century saw a surge in business literature dealing with adoption of principles from military intelligence and strategy into the business world - to create competitive advantage, to improve the business strategy of organizations. Select few publications are listed in this section.

1. [Business Warfare: A framework for Business Strategy based on Clausewitz, Sun Tzu, Foch and Machiavelli](https://www.amazon.in/Business-Warfare-framework-Clausewitz-Machiavelli/dp/1963271211): 2024, [Paulo Cardoso Do Amaral](https://www.linkedin.com/in/paulo-cardoso-do-amaral-209/)   
2. [The Influence of Military Strategies to Buisness: Skills to Help with Problem Solving & Decision Making](https://annas-archive.org/md5/56b2000466c719cbd5ce9dfeaacd301d): 2017, M. D. White
